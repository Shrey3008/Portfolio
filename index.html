About

From manufacturing data to production APIs

I'm a Master of Science in Computer Science candidate at Roosevelt University (graduating May 2026), building on a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering from Gujarat Technological University. My work sits at the intersection of full-stack development, data engineering, and applied AI.

I got my start automating security reviews and building ETL pipelines that manufacturing teams actually used — cutting hours of manual work into minutes and turning raw production data into dashboards people checked every day. That habit of turning messy processes into clean, reliable systems now shows up in how I build software: typed APIs, containerized deployments, and interfaces that get out of the user's way.

Most recently, I designed and shipped TrailPack, a full-stack SaaS app that pairs an AI recommendation engine with real-time weather data — deployed end-to-end on AWS with a one-command redeploy pipeline.
