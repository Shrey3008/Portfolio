About

From manufacturing data to production APIs

I'm a Master of Science in Computer Science graduate from Roosevelt University (May 2026), building on a Bachelor's in Computer Engineering from Gujarat Technological University. My work sits at the intersection of full-stack development, data engineering, and applied AI.

I got my start automating security reviews and building ETL pipelines that manufacturing teams actually used — cutting hours of manual work into minutes and turning raw production data into dashboards people checked every day. That habit of turning messy processes into clean, reliable systems now shows up in how I build software: typed APIs, containerized deployments, and interfaces that get out of the user's way.

Most recently, I designed and shipped TalkData, a full-stack platform that turns plain-English questions into SQL queries and live charts using a RAG-based schema retrieval pipeline — deployed end-to-end with a 3-layer security architecture and a 55-test suite validating it.
